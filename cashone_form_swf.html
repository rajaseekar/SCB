Important things to note

  • If you do not have any existing credit facility with any bank and your approved loan amount is $5,000 and above, the interest rate applicable to your CashOne loan is at 9%p.a. (Effective interest rates range from 16.17%p.a. to 18.13%p.a.).
  • An early redemption fee of S$250 per account will be charged if you prepay the loan prematurely.
  • Annual fee of $99 (first year) and $50 (second year onwards till expiry of instalment tenor) will be charged per CashOne loan account. The actual disbursement will be nett of the first-year annual fee.
  • If you are an existing Standard Chartered Credit Cardholder, your available credit card limit may be used for CashOne loan.
  • If you are an existing Standard Chartered Personal Credit customer, your line of credit may be converted to a CashOne loan.
  • If you hold any of our Credit Cards with a credit limit of $500 and your annual income ranges from $20,000 to $29,999, your Credit Card account will be closed upon approval of the CashOne.
